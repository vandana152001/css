Making Tea

  1. 1.Boil Water in a Pot

  2. 2.Add tea leaves and sugar

  3. 3.Let it simmer for a few minutes

  4. 4.Strain the tea into a cup and add milk if desired

Making Paratha and Aloo Curry

  1. 1.Knead the dough for paratha and let it rest.

  2. 2.Roll out the dough and cool on a hot griddle with oil or ghee.

  3. 3.For aloo curry , saute onions , tomatoes and spices in a pan.

  4. 4.Add boiled potatoes and cool until the curry thickens

Making Biryani

  1. 1.Marinate the chicken with alla the spices

  2. 2.cook basmati rice until its 70% done

  3. 3.Layer the rice and chicken marinate

  4. 4.Cook on low flame until fully cooked
